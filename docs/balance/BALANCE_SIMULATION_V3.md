## 5. Key Findings (v3)

### ✅ Major Improvements
- **Symmetric takedowns work** — B can now shoot takedowns, completely changing the balance landscape
- **Mirror matchups approaching complementarity** — 103-145% sum (was 177-200%)
- **Muay Thai ↔ Wrestler near-perfect** — 48% vs 55%, the ideal balance point
- **BJJ submissions now dominant** — 84% of wins by submission, realistic grappling threat

### 🟡 Observations
- **Boxer weakest** (32.6%) — striking ×1.15 can't overcome grappler takedowns when B also shoots
- **BJJ strongest** (85.5%) — BJJ ×1.50 creates unstoppable submission threat from any position
- **KO rates dropped significantly** — Boxer KO% fell from 26% to 8.7%, Muay Thai from 48% to 30%
- **Wrestler well-balanced** — 75.5% overall, good mix of sub/dec wins

### Current Balance Assessment
The engine is now **structurally symmetric**. Remaining imbalance is from stats multipliers.
If targeting ~50% across all archetypes at equal stats: Boxer needs buff, BJJ needs nerf,
Muay Thai vs non-grapplers still too strong (91% vs Boxer).
